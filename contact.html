Frequently Asked Questions

What cybersecurity services do you offer for SMEs?

We offer a comprehensive range of cybersecurity services tailored for small and medium enterprises, including online training courses, security assessments, consultation services, and customized cybersecurity solutions. Our goal is to provide affordable and effective protection for businesses of all sizes.

How can I get technical support for course-related issues?

For technical support related to our courses, you can contact our support team at support@cybershield.com or call our helpline at +123 456 7890 during business hours. Alternatively, registered users can access the support section in their dashboard where they can submit tickets and access our knowledge base.

Do you offer corporate training packages?

Yes, we offer customized corporate training packages for organizations of all sizes. Our corporate solutions include bulk course access, custom learning paths, progress tracking, and company-specific assessments. Contact our sales team at sales@cybershield.com to discuss your organization's specific needs and receive a tailored quote.

Are certificates provided upon course completion?

Yes, all our paid courses include a digital certificate of completion. These certificates are issued once you successfully complete all course modules and pass the final assessment. The certificates are verifiable and can be downloaded, printed, or shared directly from your dashboard.

How can I become a partner or affiliate?

We welcome partnerships with cybersecurity professionals, IT service providers, and educational institutions. To learn more about our partnership or affiliate programs, please email partners@cybershield.com with information about your organization and your area of interest. Our partnership team will get back to you with relevant details.
